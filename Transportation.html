Locally Getting Around The Island

Public Bus

Taniti offers a local public bus that allows you to move around the city in local routes. Bus fees vary but run all day from 5am to 11pm!

Daily ~ Low costs daily

Taxis & Ubers

Taniti offers local taxis that will vary from prices based on the length of the arrival and destination. Taniti also offers popular services such as lyft and Uber which are more convienant for late night adventures.

Avaliable 24/7 ~ Cost Varies

Book Now

Walking

Many destinations in Taniti are very walkable! Many restraunts, hotels, and attractions are at walkable distance from each other making it very easy to travel walking.

Free ~ Best in the City

Vehicle Rentals

Car Rentals

Car rentals are avaliable to any one above the age of 25 and a valid driver's license. Rental agencies are avaliable in the center of the City and one located at the Taniti airport.

Avaliable From 6am - 10pm ~ Prices Vary on Car

Book Now

Bicycle & Scooter Rentals

Bicycle and Scooter rentals will be located locally in the center of Taniti City. Each will be parked at a designated spot and must be returned to the designated spot when done.

Avaliable all Day ~ Pay per mile
